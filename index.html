Akifumi Wachi

I am a research scientist at IBM Research AI. I received B.S. and M.S. degres from Univesity of Tokyo and Ph.D. degree from University of Tsukuba. During my master's degree, I participated in the development or operation of small satellites (e.g., EQUULEUS).

Contact: akifumi.wachi [at] gmail.com

Research Interest

My core research interest is in machine learning for reliable sequential decision making for safety-critical systems. My area of focus is reinforcement learning, including the important subclass known as safe reinforcement learning.
